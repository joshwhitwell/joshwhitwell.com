Uninstall PWA

If you're experiencing issues with outdated icons or need to completely reset the PWA installation, follow these steps:

Cache cleared successfully! Now you need to uninstall the PWA from your device.

Chrome (Desktop)

  1. Open Chrome Settings (⋮ menu)
  2. Select "Apps" from the left sidebar
  3. Find this app in the list
  4. Click on the options menu (⋮) for the app
  5. Select "Uninstall"

Chrome (Android)

  1. Long press on the app icon on your home screen
  2. Select "Uninstall" or drag to the Uninstall area
  3. Go to Chrome and visit this site again

Safari (iOS)

  1. Long press on the app icon on your home screen
  2. Select "Delete App" or "Remove App"
  3. Confirm deletion
  4. Go to Safari and visit this site again

Clear Browser Cache

For a complete reset, also clear your browser's cache:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Clear browsing data
  • Safari: Settings → Safari → Clear History and Website Data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data → Clear Data

After completing these steps, refresh this page and you should see the updated icons.
